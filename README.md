## 🐛 About Me
I am an aspiring researcher and developer currently pursuing my B.Sc. in CSE at BRAC University. I work at the intersection of **Health Informatics, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Computer Vision**. My primary mission is to create affordable, human-centric health and well-being technologies designed for underrepresented communities.

* 🔬 **Current Focus:** Building an Action Recognition pipeline utilizing VQA and IoT devices to assist elderly Dementia patients.
* 🛠️ **The Daily Grind:** I'm currently running a personal challenge called **`Daily Git`** where I step away from the AI "prompt shield" and build raw coding muscle memory from scratch, one script at a time!

## 🔬 Featured Projects & Research

### 🏥 Health Tech & Wearables
* **ReMedAl**
  An AI-powered health ecosystem built to combat prescription misuse and antibiotic resistance. Features a rule-based AI chatbot, drug database engine, and automated dose monitoring.
* **Wearable Anxiety Detector (WAD)** ⏳ *In Progress*
  A multi-modal system tracking mouse cursor telemetry, typing patterns, and physiological signals (Heart Rate, EEG) to dynamically detect anxiety and trigger grounding exercises.

### 🗣️ NLP & Low-Resource Language Processing
* **Few-shot Emotion Recognition in Romanized Bengali (Banglish)**
  Evaluated 9 NLI/emotion architectures. Fine-tuned XLM-RoBERTa and DistilBERT to boost accuracy to 48.1%, pioneering the use of KAN architectures to bridge performance gaps.
* **Sarcasm Detection in Banglish**
  Implemented zero-shot and cross-lingual transfer learning algorithms to process code-mixed data.
